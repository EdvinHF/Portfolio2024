TROAX INTERNSHIP 2024

In the spring of 2024, I had the opportunity to join Troax Group as a Graphic Design/Content Creator Intern. Troax, the global leader in protective mesh panel solutions, operates across a wide range of safety solutions with a mission to safeguard people, property, and processes. With a presence in 45 countries, my main role involved creating content for both internal and external communication across the organization.

  • Content Creation
  • Graphic Design
  • Motion Graphics
  • 3D Visualization
  • Print

One project I worked on at Troax involved designing a new signage system for the Swedish factory located at the headquarters in Hillerstorp. My goal was to create a simple and unified system that stayed true to Troax’s design language while ensuring clarity and consistency. The system was color-coded to differentiate between various sign categories.

A large part of my work, especially when creating various content materials, involved using Blender to create 3D imagery and animations. Despite having little experience with 3D design prior to the internship, I quickly developed a passion for working in this medium and enjoyed exploring its creative possibilities.

The main aspect of my internship involved creating social media posts. Working within the marketing department, I collaborated closely with the team and key stakeholders to produce posts and together develop campaigns for Troax Group’s social media channels.
