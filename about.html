About

Creating Meaningful Designs
And Digital Experiences

Hi! I'm Edvin Höglin Forsberg I’m a graphic design and web development student with a deep passion for creativity and problem-solving.

Graphic design is at the heart of everything I do. I believe great design has the power to tell a story, evoke emotion, and leave a lasting impression. From branding and typography to content creation and UI design, I thrive on creating visuals that are striking, meaningful, and uniquely tailored to each project. While graphic design fuels my creativity, web development allows me to dive into the technical side of design, giving me a broader perspective on brands and marketing as a whole. I enjoy building seamless, visually engaging user experiences that balance form and function. This dual approach in design and development enables me to bring a holistic approach to my work, ensuring every project is both polished and impactful.

Whether it’s crafting a brand identity, designing a website, or creating a complete digital experience, I’m dedicated to turning your vision into something unforgettable. Let’s make something remarkable together!ツ
