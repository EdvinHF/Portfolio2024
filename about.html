About

Creating Connections Through Creativity & Purpose

Hi! I'm Edvin Höglin Forsberg.

I’m a graphic designer based in Jönköping with a recently completed degree in graphic design and web development. My main focus within the field of graphic design is on branding, content creation, and motion graphics, areas where my goals are to create unique and forward-thinking visuals that connect with people.

During my studies and early career, I’ve had the opportunity to collaborate with companies like Troax, Elbit Systems, and Hugos Chark. These projects have helped me grow both creatively and technically while teaching me how to adapt design thinking to different industries and goals. In 2025, I was awarded a diploma from Jönköpings Borgarekassa & Bjuggska fonden, an encouraging recognition that continues to motivate me in my creative journey

In addition to graphic design, I have hands-on experience in web development and more technical aspects of design, which allows me to bring ideas to life across a range of mediums. Whether I’m working on print, digital, or interactive projects, I value the ability to move seamlessly between form and function. .

Ultimately, what drives me is to create designs that distinguish themselves through creativity and innovation.
