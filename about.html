About

Hi! I’m Edvin Höglin Forsberg, a graphic designer from Jönköping with a degree in graphic design and web development. I specialize in branding, content creation, and motion graphics, aiming to create visuals that feel unique, thoughtful, and memorable.

During my early career, I’ve had the chance to collaborate with companies such as Troax, Elbit Systems, and Hugos Chark, experiences that sharpened both my creative and technical skills and taught me to adapt design thinking to different industries. In 2025 I was honored with a diploma from Jönköpings Borgarekassa & Bjuggska fonden, a recognition that continues to inspire my work.

Alongside graphic design, I have practical web development experience, allowing me to move smoothly between print, digital, and interactive projects while balancing creativity with functionality.
