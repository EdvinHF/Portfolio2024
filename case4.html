HIVE MIND BRAND CREATION

Hivemind originated from an idea my friends and I had about starting a drone show company. However, after realizing the financial challenges such a venture would entail, we decided to scrap the plan. Still, I was inspired by the concept and wanted to explore the brand visually, so I created Hivemind and began designing its visual components.
