Nomu

NOMU BRAND CREATION

The Nomu branding project was a collaborative effort as part of the Content Creation course held by Ny Studio at Jönköping University. We developed a sake brand with the goal of completely differentiating it from competitors by creating a vibrant identity aimed at a younger European market. The project included developing a comprehensive brand identity, visuals, packaging design, imagery, and a social media campaign with tailored approaches to suit the unique dynamics of different social media platforms.
